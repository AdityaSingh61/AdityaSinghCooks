Cook · Builder · Age 14

I've been cooking since I was three, and building robots more recently — here's how I think about both.

Two different worlds that turn out to run on the same instincts: try something, see what happens, adjust, try again.

Precision, iteration, and creativity are the throughline. A recipe and a robot are both systems you refine over and over until they work exactly right — one measured in grams and minutes, the other in milliseconds and milliamps.
